Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to coordinate schedules with friends and acquaintances, to allow any member of a group to propose an activity that every member can attend, and to carry notifications of said proposals to other members of the group.

Audience

The audience consists of everyone 12 and older. The idea is to reach out to groups of casually connected individuals, rather than formal clubs where people are free to join or leave. Most people may interact with this on a mobile device. Some of the group meetings may be between different kinds of people.
